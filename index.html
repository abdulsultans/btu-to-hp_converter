BTU to Horsepower
Converter







BTU stands for "British Thermal Units."
In the context of air conditioners, it is a unit of measurement that refers
to the amount of heat the HVAC unit can remove from the room in an hour.
The higher the BTU, the more powerful the AC system is and the more cooling capacity it has.
This means a higher BTU-rated AC can cool a larger space compared to systems with lower BTU ratings.
